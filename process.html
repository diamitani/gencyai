Process & Methodology

The ROSTR Framework.

We don't just build software. We extract the core intent from your SOPs and translate them into deterministic, AI-powered systems.

Pipeline
SOP → Intent → Agent.
Our proprietary methodology for turning manual, human-driven standard operating procedures into scalable AI architecture.
STEP 01

JTBD Analysis

We break down your SOP using Jobs-To-Be-Done theory. We interview operators to understand the exact job, the required inputs, the success criteria, and the edge cases.

STEP 02

Natural Language Extraction

Our NLP assistant digests the JTBD data and extracts pure intent. This intent is translated into logical flowcharts, prompt architectures, and tool requirements.

STEP 03

ROSTR Build

We deploy the final agent or workflow using the ROSTR system. The agent is strictly bounded by deterministic rules, ensuring it executes the SOP flawlessly every time. Read the ROSTR Whitepaper ↗
